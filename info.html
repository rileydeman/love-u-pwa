General Information

What is ❤️U Festival?

The ❤️U Festival is for (new) students in the Utrecht region and is an addition to UIT.

Location

Strijkviertel, Utrecht

Navigation address: Strijkviertel Weg, Utrecht

Festival Times

Saturday: 11:30 — 03:30
Sunday: 10:45 — 00:00

Golden GLU

Students of the GLU have special privileges during the festival and can be recognized by a gold bracelet.

With this gold bracelet they can use the gold toilets and gold marked order points at the bars during the festival without having to stand in a queue.

Transport & Accessibility

Bike

There is a large free bicycle shed where you can park your bike all day.

Car

You can purchase a parking ticket. You can park at P+R Papendorp, follow the signs 'P online ticket'. Did you not purchase a ticket in advance? Then you can purchase a parking ticket from the parking attendant on location (PIN ONLY). Please note: FULL=FULL

Public Transportation

Are you coming to Lief by public transport? Then plan your trip via 9292.nl.

Shuttlebus

From Utrecht Central Station you can take our free shuttle bus to the festival grounds. You can find this bus at the central station on Mineurslaan. Follow the white signs with black arrows and '❤️U Festival'.

The bus runs between 11:00 and 19:00 on Saturday and between 11:15 and 19:00 on Sunday towards the festival and from 21:00 you can get on again to go to the station.

Taxi + Kiss & Ride

Navigate to Strijkviertel, De Meern (Utrecht). Follow the signs "Kiss & Ride ❤️U Festival", once you are near the festival grounds.

FAQ

I use medication. What now?

You are allowed to take medication in a dose that you need for 1 day. A doctor's certificate/medication passport is required. Security will assess your documentation and check the medication. It is possible that the first aid will take your medication (for example if it is dangerous in combination with alcohol) into custody and you can only take it at the first aid.

Can I leave the festival grounds during the event?

No, unfortunately that is not possible. In order to guarantee the safety of all visitors, we cannot allow the festival site to be left in between. We have no insight into what a visitor does outside the festival site and in what state they enter the site again. Therefore, no exceptions can be made for this. We have enough lounge areas, food stands & bars to last a whole day.

Are there lockers?

There are lockers on the festival grounds where you can safely store your belongings!

These can fit 3 to 4 coats. Good to know: you can open and close your locker as often as you like throughout the day.

It is not possible to reserve a locker online.
